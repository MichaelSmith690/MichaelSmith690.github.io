Dan's Fans City


About


Dan’s Fan City is America’s independent ceiling fan retailer. Dan’s Fan City’s challenge involved increasing their online sales. Most of their sales were done in the brick-and-mortar stores, but the online store sales were not keeping up. One major contributor to this was that their previous CMS was antiquated and did not support content customization and personalization.


What/How did?


The updated website is now a reflection of a modern e-commerce store. With enhanced backed-end SEO, the site is seeing better search engine rankings and an increase in organic visitors. Also, the fan builder plugin is one of the best on the market and will help drive Dan’s Fan City online sales goals.

Technology Stack

  • Language: TypeScript, Javascript, HTML, SCSS
  • Framework: NodeJS, Angular4
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Web Services: Node RESTful API
  • Payment Gateway: Paypal
  • Server: AWS
  • Others: Amazon Product Advertising API, HTML Scraping, Social network login, MailChimp for email tracking
